Password Reset Guide

If you are unable to log in to your account, follow these steps to reset your password.

Step 1: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password".
Step 2: Enter your registered email address and click Submit.
Step 3: Check your inbox for a password reset email. It arrives within 2 minutes.
Step 4: Click the reset link in the email. The link expires in 30 minutes.
Step 5: Enter a new password. It must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase letter and one number.
Step 6: Log in with your new password.

Common Issues:
- Reset email not received: Check your spam/junk folder. If still missing, ensure you are using your registered email address.
- Link expired: Request a new reset link from the login page.
- Account locked: After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes. Contact support if the issue persists.
- 2FA issues: If two-factor authentication is blocking access, contact support with your account email and government ID for manual verification.

For accounts that cannot be recovered through self-service, email support@company.com with subject "Account Recovery Request".
