Login Troubleshooting Guide

If you are experiencing issues logging in, follow this guide before contacting support.

Issue 1: Incorrect Password
Solution: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.

Issue 2: Account Locked
Cause: 5 or more failed login attempts lock the account for 30 minutes.
Solution: Wait 30 minutes and try again, or contact support for immediate unlock.

Issue 3: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Not Working
- Ensure your authenticator app time is synced correctly.
- Use a backup code if you have lost access to your authenticator device.
- If all backup codes are used, contact support with your registered email and ID.

Issue 4: Browser Issues
- Clear browser cache and cookies, then try again.
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
- Disable browser extensions, especially ad blockers.
- Try incognito/private browsing mode.

Issue 5: SSO Login Failure (Enterprise)
- Confirm your organization's SSO is correctly configured by your IT admin.
- Verify that your email domain is whitelisted in your SSO provider settings.
- Check with your IT team that your account is active in the identity provider.

Issue 6: Page Not Loading
- Check your internet connection.
- Try accessing from a different network or device.
- Visit our status page at status.company.com to check for outages.

If none of these steps resolve your issue, contact support@company.com with your account email, browser version, and a screenshot of any error message.
