Pricing Plans and Features

Starter Plan — $29/month
- Up to 3 team members
- 10GB cloud storage
- Basic analytics dashboard
- Email support (48-hour response time)
- API access: 1,000 requests/day
- Suitable for freelancers and small teams

Professional Plan — $79/month
- Up to 15 team members
- 50GB cloud storage
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Priority email and chat support (8-hour response time)
- API access: 10,000 requests/day
- Webhook integrations
- Custom branding
- Suitable for growing businesses

Enterprise Plan — $199/month
- Unlimited team members
- 500GB cloud storage
- Full analytics suite with export
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone, email, and chat support (1-hour response SLA)
- API access: 100,000 requests/day
- SSO and SAML integration
- Custom contract and invoicing
- SLA with 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Suitable for large organizations

Plan Upgrades:
You can upgrade at any time from Account Settings > Billing. The new plan activates immediately and you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

Plan Downgrades:
Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Data exceeding new plan limits must be reduced before downgrade.

Free Trial:
A 14-day free trial of the Professional Plan is available for new accounts. No credit card required.
