Account Suspension Policy

Why Accounts Get Suspended:

1. Payment Failure: If a payment fails and is not resolved within 7 days, the account is suspended. All data is retained for 30 days during suspension.

2. Terms of Service Violation: Accounts that violate our Terms of Service are suspended immediately. Common violations include:
   - Sending spam through our platform
   - Exceeding API rate limits repeatedly
   - Using the service for illegal activities
   - Unauthorized reselling of access

3. Security Risk: If suspicious activity is detected (e.g., compromised credentials, unusual login locations), accounts may be temporarily suspended to protect your data.

How to Reactivate a Suspended Account:

Payment Failure Suspension:
- Update your payment method in Account Settings > Billing > Payment Methods.
- Outstanding balance will be charged automatically.
- Account reactivates within 1 hour of successful payment.

Terms of Service Suspension:
- Email appeals@company.com with subject "Account Suspension Appeal".
- Include your account email and a description of the situation.
- Appeals are reviewed within 3 business days.

Security Suspension:
- Contact support@company.com to verify your identity.
- You will be asked to confirm recent account activity.

Data Retention:
Suspended accounts retain all data for 30 days. After 30 days, data may be permanently deleted. Export your data before suspension takes effect.
