Billing and Refund Policy

Subscription Plans:
- Starter Plan: $29/month — up to 3 users, 10GB storage
- Professional Plan: $79/month — up to 15 users, 50GB storage
- Enterprise Plan: $199/month — unlimited users, 500GB storage, SLA guarantee

Payment Methods:
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. All transactions are processed securely via Stripe.

Billing Cycle:
Subscriptions renew automatically on the same date each month. You will receive an invoice via email 3 days before each renewal.

Refund Policy:
- Refunds are available within 7 days of the initial purchase only.
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after the 7-day window.
- Annual plans can be refunded within 30 days pro-rated for unused months.
- Refund requests must be submitted via email to billing@company.com.

Cancellation:
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Access continues until end of the current billing period.

Disputed Charges:
If you believe you were charged incorrectly, email billing@company.com within 14 days with your invoice number. Disputes are resolved within 5 business days.

Tax:
Applicable taxes are added based on your billing address. VAT customers in the EU must provide a valid VAT number to be exempted.
