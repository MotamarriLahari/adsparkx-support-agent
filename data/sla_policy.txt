Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

This document applies to Enterprise Plan customers only.

Uptime Guarantee:
We guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime for all core services. This equates to no more than 43.8 minutes of downtime per month.

Downtime Definition:
Downtime is defined as the service being completely unavailable for more than 5 consecutive minutes. Scheduled maintenance windows are excluded.

Scheduled Maintenance:
Maintenance is performed on the first Sunday of each month between 2:00 AM and 4:00 AM UTC. Customers are notified 7 days in advance via email.

SLA Credits:
If uptime falls below 99.9%, customers are entitled to service credits:
- 99.0% to 99.9% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 95.0% to 99.0% uptime: 25% credit of monthly fee
- Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly fee

Credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident via billing@company.com.

Support Response Times (Enterprise):
- Critical (service down): 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution target
- High (major feature broken): 4-hour response, 8-hour resolution target
- Medium (minor issue): 8-hour response, 24-hour resolution target
- Low (general questions): 24-hour response

Status Page:
Current and historical uptime is available at status.company.com. Subscribe to status updates to receive notifications of incidents.
