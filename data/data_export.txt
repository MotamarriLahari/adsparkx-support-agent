Data Export Guide

You can export your account data at any time from the dashboard.

What Can Be Exported:
- User and team data (CSV)
- Project and task data (CSV or JSON)
- Analytics reports (CSV or PDF)
- Billing history and invoices (PDF)
- API usage logs (JSON)

How to Export:
1. Go to Account Settings > Data > Export.
2. Select the data type you want to export.
3. Choose the date range (optional).
4. Select format: CSV, JSON, or PDF.
5. Click "Request Export".
6. You will receive an email with a download link within 15 minutes.
7. The download link expires after 24 hours.

Large Exports:
Exports over 1GB are split into multiple files. All files are included in the same download email as a ZIP archive.

Automated Exports:
Professional and Enterprise customers can schedule automated exports via Settings > Data > Scheduled Exports. Choose daily, weekly, or monthly frequency.

GDPR Data Requests:
For personal data export requests under GDPR, email privacy@company.com with subject "GDPR Data Request". Requests are fulfilled within 30 days as required by law.

Data Deletion:
To permanently delete your account data, go to Account Settings > Data > Delete Account. This action is irreversible. All data is deleted within 72 hours.
